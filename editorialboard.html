Join as Editorial Board Member

The Institute Enterprise and Managemnt Analytics (IEMA) invites qualified professionals to join our esteemed editorial board. As an editorial board member, you will play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and integrity of our publications.

Responsibilities:

  • Review submitted manuscripts in your area of expertise
  • Provide constructive feedback to authors
  • Participate in editorial board meetings
  • Suggest topics for special issues
  • Promote the journal within your professional network

Benefits:

  • Enhance your academic profile and CV
  • Network with international experts in your field
  • Early access to cutting-edge research
  • Certificate of recognition
  • Opportunity to shape the direction of research in your discipline

Important Information

- Editorial board members are typically appointed for a 2-year term

- We expect board members to review at least 3-5 manuscripts per year

- All applications will be reviewed by the editorial committee within 4-6 weeks
